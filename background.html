Company Background

BELERON Enterprises is owned by the researcher’s grandfather and was built in the year 1998 therefore making it 23 years old now. The name BELERON was derived from the names of Ben (The researcher’s Grandfather), Leticia (The researcher’s Grandmother) and Veronica also known as Ron (The researcher’s mother.) BELERON Enterprises is a Woodworks shop. These are the following items the business offers:
  • Panel/French/Flush Doors
  • Door & Window Jambs - Louvers
  • Handrails
  • Balusters
  • Iron & Steel Works
  • Steel Casement
  • Windows
  • Gates
  • Fences
  • Grills
  • All kinds of steel working
Since 2004, BELERON Enterprises has been one of AMANPULO's contractors. The company sells Sunbathing Beds, Big Wooden Umbrellas, Picnic Tables, and Door Frames, which may be found on the AMANPULO website. Because of the high quality of the goods, BELERON Enterprises has continued to get contracts from AMANPULO. BELERON Enterprises also supplies Casa Designa, Excalibur, SMI Builders, Draco Builder, and other companies in Ayala Alabang. The company is always working on new initiatives.


Existing System and Design

BELERON Enterprises continues to use a manual approach for all transactions and client communication. The company does not additionally implement an automatic method of saving or backing up data, as well as creating papers or invoices without the need of paper.

Figure 1.1 Existing System and Design

The figure shows how the company issues an invoice and an official receipt manually using a pen and paper. The employee will provide a blank order form this will serve as the invoice including the details of the order and gives the official receipt. This forms/documents that will be issued to the client each has a copy. The employee will calculate the total amount and discount if existing.
