General Objective

The overall goal of this research is to develop an online system that handles automatic transactions or communication with the client and manager, as well as storing or backing up files and generating documents or quotations. A mobile application that the client/admin can use to navigate around BELERON Enterprises products or inquiries.


Specific Objectives

The specific objectives of the study are the following:
  • Produce a user-friendly interface for clients.
  • Create an easier and faster way of inquiring products for clients.
  • Creation of invoice is easier and faster for employees and manager.
  • Accessing and retrieving of documents for employees and manager will be easy since it is stored in an organized way.
  • Easily manage all pending/completed inquiries and invoices for clients and employees.
  • Transact, communicate or inquire directly to each client or employees using the provided message function in the application.
